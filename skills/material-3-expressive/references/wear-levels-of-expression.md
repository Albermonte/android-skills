# Wear OS Levels of Expression

Source URL:
https://developer.android.com/design/ui/wear/guides/levels-of-expression

Scope
Defines three levels of expressiveness for Wear OS UI and the characteristics of each level.

Key points
- Foundational: Follows all design principles, prioritizes simplicity, clarity, and accessibility.
- Excellent: Builds on foundational with expressive color, typography, and shapes; components are flexible and responsive to context.
- Transformative: Pushes boundaries by embracing asymmetry, bold motion, and dynamic layouts while still preserving usability and accessibility.
- The levels scale expressiveness from safe to bold, helping teams tune intensity by product context.

How to use
- Use these levels to calibrate expressive intensity in a product brief.
- Align team decisions by naming the target level for each screen or flow.
